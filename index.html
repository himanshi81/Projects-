Nelson Mandela

"I learned that courage was not the absence of fear, but the triumph over it. The brave man is not he who does not feel afraid, but he who conquers that fear."

Nelson Mandela is best known for his role as a prominent anti-apartheid revolutionary, political leader and philanthropist.
He served as the President of South Africa from 1994-1999.
Nelson Mandela was born into the clan in the village of Mvezo,
in the eastern Cape, on 18th July, 1918.
Nelson Mandela fought heroically throughout his life and passed away
on 5th December, 2013.

"Long Walk to Freedom" is the autobiography of Nelson Mandela.
It was first published in 1994. The book provides details of his
early life, education and his imprisonment of 27 years.
